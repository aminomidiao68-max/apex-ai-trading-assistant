## English
### Title
APEX AI - Crypto & Forex Trading Assistant

### Short Description
Signal workflows, risk control, journaling, backtesting, and analytics in one mobile app.

### Full Description
APEX AI is a mobile-first trading assistant for crypto and forex markets, designed to combine analysis workflows, risk controls, trade journaling, backtesting, and strategy validation in one structured product.

Features:
- Live dashboard
- Signal center
- Candlestick chart
- Trade journal
- Backtesting and walk-forward analysis
- Performance analytics
- Execution preview workflows
- Risk controls and settings
